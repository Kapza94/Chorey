# Chorey Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose

Chorey is a family chore and allowance app that helps parents teach children a fixed 40 / 40 / 20 money habit: 40% Spend, 40% Savings, and 20% Giving.

Chorey is a virtual family ledger. It does not move real money in v1. Parents approve chores and settle money outside the app weekly or monthly.

## Users

- Parents create households, add children, create chores, approve work, settle buckets, manage Giving options, and manage subscription.
- Children complete chores, see their own balances, use a Spend wishlist, choose Giving destinations, and receive private progress recognition.

## Strategic Principles

- The 40 / 40 / 20 split is fixed and central to the brand.
- Children never see billing or upgrade prompts.
- Giving is human and should not be meaningfully paywalled.
- Paid features monetize household scale, automation, proof, and convenience.
- Chorey should reduce parent mental load without noisy gamification.
- Privacy matters, especially around temporary proof photos.

## Tone

- Clean, warm, trustworthy, and family-friendly.
- Parent surface: calm, factual, modern.
- Child surface: warmer, larger, quietly satisfying.
- No hype, no aggressive gamification, no childish clutter.

## Anti-References

- Not a banking app with real money movement.
- Not a social app.
- Not a leaderboard or XP game.
- Not glassmorphism, heavy gradients, or decorative noise.
- Not a child-facing paywall.
